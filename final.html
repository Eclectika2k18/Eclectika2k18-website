About Us

Eclectika 18

Eclectika , the cultural festival of NIT Raipur is widely renowned as the largest fest in central India. With a participation roster exceeding over 1 hundred colleges across the academic demographic of India, Eclectika is a glorious rendition of the ideals of innovation, conception and determination to achieve high heights of success, and is a phenomenal platform to nurture and inspire talent, while uniting a wide and diverse participation pool under a single banner.

The 2018 Programme for Eclectika is poised to exceed all expectations in keeping with its strong heritage and excelling in it's core strength as a talent platform to drive excellence.

We look forward to have an enriching experience with all the participants. We can promise it will be entertaining too!
